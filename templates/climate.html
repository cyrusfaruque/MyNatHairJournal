How does your regional climate effect your hair moisture?

It turns out that where you live has a huge affect on the level of moisture in your hair. It is vital that you always maintain a high level of moisture in your hair because without such moisture black hair is extremely susceptible to breakage. Below is a chart to illusture the affect of climate on ones hair. The humidity chart illustrates how temperature affects the level of humidity.

If you live in an extremely dry climate you can do the following things:

  • Condition your hair more frequently
  • Try Oil based Products
  • Shampoo your hair less
  • Invest in a humidifier
