Welcome to the Natural Hair Movement

So you've decided you want to go "natural", what exactly does that mean? Technically it means to be chemical-free (like relaxers and perms). But, ultimately, this movement is about you, as a black woman, allowing yourself to be free with your hair and identity. Because many black people are descendants of slaves, they were taken from their African roots and traditions. Many of these traditions were oriented in hair. Additionally, due to European standards of beauty, the view of typically kinky black hair began to be seen as ugly or unattractive. This racism led to black woman relaxing or chemically straightening their hair to fit beauty standard. Nonetheless, today is a new day! Today we embrace all kinds of hair kinks, and the beauty within the black woman's hair. We invite you to learn more about the nuances of your hair, and be empowered by your natural beauty.

Happy learning!
