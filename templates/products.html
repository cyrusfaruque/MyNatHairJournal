Becoming a Product Junkie:

Newly-Natural's Product Starter Pack:

Shampoo:

Find a shampoo that is sulfate free. Sulfates dry out one's hair, so to retain moisture, look for sulfate free products.

Conditioner:

Conditioner is key to a new Natural. Not only is it vital to maintain healthy long hair, but its also super norishing for the hair. Find a conditioner that fits your particular hair type's needs. Whether it be an ultra hydrating conditioner (creamy consistancy), or a lighter condiitioner to make the hair follicies stronger

Leave-In Conditioner:

Although it is important to find a good conditioner it is also vital to have a leave-in conditioner to keep your hair healthy after washing it. Such leave-in conditioners should be light in consistancy, and super absorbant.

Deep Conditioner:

A MUST HAVE! Deep Conditioners should be used on a bi-monthly basis. They are super insentive treatments that rebuild damaged hair, and overall promote growth and lock in moisture

Butter/Moisturizer/Twisting Curl:

This is a product that will help you style your hair. Such twisting creams or butter assist with keeping moisture while you where your various hair styles

Oil:

An oil helps seal your hair. Find an oil that is light if you have finer hair (coconut oil). A heavier oil that many new naturals love is called Black Castor Oil!
